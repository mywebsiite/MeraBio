Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Beauty

Mera Bio was born from a deep love for Moroccan beauty traditions — the hammams, the rose gardens, the argan groves that have nourished skin for generations. Every product is a bridge between ancestral wisdom and modern self-care.

We source our ingredients directly from Moroccan cooperatives, ensuring the highest quality while supporting the women who have preserved these traditions for centuries.

Your Daily Ritual

The Art of Self-Care

Transform your daily routine into a moment of luxury with our three-step ritual rooted in centuries of Moroccan beauty tradition.

1

Exfoliate

Begin with a generous application of our body scrub on damp skin. Massage in circular motions to reveal smooth, radiant skin beneath.

2

Soak

Dissolve a handful of bath salts in warm water. Close your eyes, breathe deeply, and let the botanicals melt away the day's tension.

3

Nourish

Finish with a few drops of our argan hair oil or botanical face oil. Press gently into skin or hair, sealing in moisture and radiance.
